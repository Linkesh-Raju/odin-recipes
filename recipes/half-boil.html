Half Boil

Click Here to go to HOME PAGE

Ingredients

  • 1-raw egg
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper

Steps:-

  1. Keep a pan filled with water on the stove and let it boil.
  2. Once the water starts boiling, add salt and black pepper to it.
  3. Gently add the egg to the boiling water.
  4. Let it boil for 4 minutes on medium flame.
  5. After 4 minutes, take the egg out and put it in cold water.
  6. Peel the shell off and your half boil egg is ready to eat...
