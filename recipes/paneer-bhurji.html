Paneer Bhurju

Click Here to go to HOME PAGE

Ingredients

  • 200gms chopped-Paneer
  • 1 - chopper onion
  • coriander power
  • garam masala
  • chilli powder
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • table salt
  • 1 - chopper tomato

Steps:-

  1. keep a non stick pan on the stove and pour 2 table spoon of oil
  2. after a minute add the chopped onion, salt and the ginger-garlic paste to the pan.
  3. roast it untill it turns into Golden Browncolour.
  4. add water,chilli powder,garam masala,paneer and coriander powder.
  5. mix it well till there is no water.
  6. Ready to eat...
