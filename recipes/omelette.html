Omelette

Click Here to go to HOME PAGE

Ingredients

  • 1-raw egg
  • 1/2 -chopper onion
  • 1- green chilli
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper

Steps:-

  1. Break the egg in a bowl.
  2. Add salt and black pepper to it.
  3. Chop the onion and green chilli and add it to the bowl.
  4. Mix it well with a fork.
  5. Keep a non stick pan on the stove and pour 1 table spoon of oil.
  6. After a minute add the egg mixture to the pan.
  7. Let it cook for 2 minutes on medium flame.
  8. Flip it and cook for another 2 minutes.
  9. Ready to eat...
